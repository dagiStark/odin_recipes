Burger

This is the recipe of how to make best Burger Sauce

Whisk all the ingredients together, then chill in the fridge for at least an hour before serving. That's it! You'll find the full, step-by-step recipe below.

ingredients

  • Condiments
  • Seasonings
  • Hot sauce
  • Vinegar

Steps

  1. Whisk together mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard, onion, garlic, and vinegar in a bowl. Season with hot sauce and seasoned pepper.
  2. Cover and chill at least 1 hour before serving.
